About Reach Flow AI

Reach Flow AI was born on the Sunshine Coast with one simple mission: help small and medium businesses grow smarter through automation and AI.

Founded by Alessandro Elena (aka Alex), a Data & AI professional with a passion for innovation and business development, Reach Flow AI combines intelligent automation with human precision.

We optimise communication, office management, and customer support—reducing operational costs while improving efficiency. Our hybrid model blends human insight with AI automation, offering results faster and at a fraction of the cost of a traditional assistant.

Every client receives weekly dashboards comparing traditional workloads with our AI-enhanced workflow, clearly showing time and cost savings through intuitive charts.
